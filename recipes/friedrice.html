Easy Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is the ultimate family-friendly dish that yields maximum flavor without fuss.

Loaded with tender sauteed veggies and delicious bits of scrambled egg, this top-rated recipe makes it easy to recreate a takeout favorite from home in just 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Combine 4 cups of water and 2 cups of white rice in a saucepan; bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat, then cover and simmer until the rice is tender and the water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Remove from the heat and let cool to room temperature.
